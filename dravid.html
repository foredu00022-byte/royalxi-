Biography

Rahul Dravid was one of the most technically sound batsmen in cricket history. Known for patience and discipline, he rescued India countless times.

Career Highlights

Dravid scored over 13,000 Test runs and served Indian cricket both as captain and later as a successful coach.

Why Underrated

Overshadowed by aggressive stars, his calm excellence often went unnoticed.

Playing Style

Defensive mastery, flawless technique, and unmatched concentration.
