One of the Greatest All-Rounders in Cricket History

Biography

Jacques Kallis is regarded as one of the most complete cricketers ever. He dominated world cricket with both bat and ball for nearly two decades.

Career

With over 10,000 runs and 250+ wickets in both Tests and ODIs, Kallis set unmatched standards for all-round excellence.

Why Underrated

His calm nature meant he never sought attention, despite achievements greater than many legends.

Playing Style

Technically perfect batting combined with powerful seam bowling.
